Discover careers in the Civil Service

The Civil Service is changing. We’re finding new ways to serve the public, work more efficiently and make government more transparent. And we’re making the Civil Service a truly great place to work.

Our Departments

Running the UK is complicated. That’s why the Civil Service is divided into departments, which deal with areas such as education, health and defence. A lot of departments will have their own agencies dealing with specialist subjects.

Whatever your passion – from the environment to culture, there’s a department or agency to match.

Our Professions

Every Civil Service job is also part of a ‘profession’ – everything from legal and IT to HR or service delivery. Your profession offers networking opportunities and training and development programmes – plus the chance to work on major cross-government projects.

Discover professions

Where you'll find us

We’re everywhere. You’ll find locations right across the UK – a far cry from the cliché of the bowler-hatted Westminster civil servant. In fact, just 18% of our jobs are based in London.
